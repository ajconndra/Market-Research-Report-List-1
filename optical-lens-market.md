<p><ul><li>Resin Lens</li><li>Optical Glass Lens</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The optical lens market is primarily divided into two main types: resin lenses and optical glass lenses. Resin lenses are lighter, impact-resistant, and often more affordable, making them popular in everyday eyewear. They offer various coatings for UV protection and scratch resistance. On the other hand, optical glass lenses provide superior clarity, durability, and scratch resistance, appealing to high-end markets like photography and precision instruments. Each type serves distinct consumer needs, driving innovation and competition in the market.</p></p>
