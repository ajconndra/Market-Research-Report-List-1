<p><ul><li>Integrated Photosensitive Drum</li><li>Non-Integrated Photosensitive Drum</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The photosensitive drum market is categorized into two main types: integrated and non-integrated photosensitive drums. Integrated photosensitive drums combine the imaging drum and toner cartridge into a single unit, streamlining installation and maintenance, and often enhancing print quality. In contrast, non-integrated photosensitive drums are standalone components, requiring separate toner cartridges. This separation allows for more flexibility in toner choices and potential cost savings, appealing to users with diverse printing needs. Both types play vital roles in the printing industry.</p></p>
