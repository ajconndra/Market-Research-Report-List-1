<p><ul><li>Radial</li><li>Axial</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The single-phase capacitors market is primarily segmented into radial and axial types. Radial capacitors have leads coming out from one end, making them suitable for applications where space is limited and vertical mounting is preferred. Axial capacitors, with leads extending from both ends, offer better stability and are typically used in applications requiring a longer circuit path or where space is less constrained. Both types cater to diverse needs in power supply, motor control, and energy storage applications.</p></p>
