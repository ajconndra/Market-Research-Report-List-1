<p><p>The biopolymer painting market encompasses various types, including Bio PU, Bio PA, Bio PBS, and Starch painting. Bio PU (Polyurethane) offers flexibility and durability for coatings, making it suitable for various applications. Bio PA (Polyamide) provides high thermal resistance and strength, ideal for performance coatings. Bio PBS (Polybutylene Succinate) is biodegradable and suitable for eco-friendly applications. Starch painting utilizes natural starch as a binder, promoting environmental sustainability. Together, these biopolymers contribute to a more sustainable and eco-conscious paint industry.</p></p>
