<p><p>The water-based pharmaceutical ink market encompasses three primary types: solids, semi-solids, and liquids. Solid inks are typically used for applications requiring high durability and adhesion, often utilized in packaging and labeling. Semi-solid inks offer a balance between viscosity and application ease, suitable for screen printing and more intricate designs. Liquid inks are versatile, allowing for smooth application on various surfaces and suitable for large-scale printing. Each type caters to specific pharmaceutical needs, ensuring optimal performance and compliance.</p></p>
