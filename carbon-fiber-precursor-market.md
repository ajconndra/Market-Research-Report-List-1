<p><p>The carbon fiber precursor market is primarily categorized into three types: PAN-based, coal pitch-based, and other types. PAN-based precursors, derived from polyacrylonitrile, dominate the market due to their high tensile strength and suitability for various applications. Coal pitch-based precursors utilize coal tar pitch, which offers a cost-effective alternative but with lower mechanical properties. Other types include rayon and lignin-based precursors, which are less common but explored for niche applications. Each type plays a crucial role in the performance and cost-effectiveness of carbon fibers.</p></p>
