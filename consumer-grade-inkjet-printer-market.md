<p><strong>The Consumer Grade Inkjet Printer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Piezoelectric Type</li><li>Thermal Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The consumer grade inkjet printer market is primarily categorized into two types: piezoelectric and thermal. Piezoelectric printers use a piezoelectric crystal to control ink droplet ejection, offering precise placement and compatibility with a wider range of ink types. Conversely, thermal inkjet printers heat the ink to create vapor bubbles, which propel droplets onto the paper. Thermal printers are often more common and cost-effective, while piezoelectric models tend to provide better print quality and durability. Each type serves different user needs and preferences.</p></p>
