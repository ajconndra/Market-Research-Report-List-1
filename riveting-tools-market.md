Battery-operated riveting tools offer portability and convenience, allowing users to work without being tethered to a power source, making them suitable for various job sites. Hand-operated riveting tools, typically more straightforward and cost-effective, are user-controlled and best for smaller tasks or DIY projects. Each tool type serves distinct operational needs and preferences.</p></p>
