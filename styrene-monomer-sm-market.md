<p><p>The Styrene Monomer (SM) market is primarily driven by two production methods: the Ethylbenzene Dehydrogenation Method and the Ethylbenzene Oxidation Method. The dehydrogenation method involves heating ethylbenzene to convert it directly into styrene, offering higher yields and efficiency. Conversely, the oxidation method converts ethylbenzene to styrene through a series of chemical reactions involving oxygen, typically resulting in higher purity levels. Both processes cater to the growing demand in industries like plastics, automotive, and consumer goods.</p></p>
