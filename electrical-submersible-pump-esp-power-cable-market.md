<p><p>The Electrical Submersible Pump (ESP) power cable market features two key types: EPDM (Ethylene Propylene Diene Monomer) and polypropylene cables. EPDM offers exceptional resistance to temperature fluctuations, oils, and chemicals, making it ideal for harsh environments. It also maintains flexibility and durability under various conditions. In contrast, polypropylene cables are lightweight, resistant to moisture, and possess good insulation properties, making them suitable for submersible applications. Both materials enhance the reliability and efficiency of ESP systems in diverse industries.</p></p>
