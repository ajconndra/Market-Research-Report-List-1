<p><p>The Polyvinyl Chloride (PVC) Paste Resin market is primarily categorized into two production methods: the Micro Suspension Method and the Emulsion Method. The Micro Suspension Method involves creating small PVC particles suspended in a liquid medium, resulting in excellent clarity and higher performance applications, often used in films and coatings. Conversely, the Emulsion Method produces resin by dispersing PVC particles in an aqueous medium, offering good flexibility and viscosity control, making it suitable for various sealants and adhesives. Both methods cater to specific industry requirements and applications.</p></p>
