<p><p>The photopolymer resin market encompasses various types, including onium salts, which act as photoinitiators facilitating the curing process upon exposure to light. Organometallic compounds serve as catalysts to enhance polymerization reactions. Pyridinium salts are another class of photoinitiators that also help initiate curing. Oligomers and monomers are essential components that form the backbone of photopolymers, contributing to their final properties. Additionally, other market segments may include custom formulations and specialty resins tailored for specific applications in industries like printing, coatings, and dental.</p></p>
