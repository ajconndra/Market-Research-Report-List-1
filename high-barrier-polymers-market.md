<p><p>High barrier polymers are crucial for packaging materials that require excellent barrier properties to protect contents from gases, moisture, and contaminants. EVOH (ethylene vinyl alcohol) high barrier polymers are known for their superior gas barrier characteristics, making them ideal for food packaging. PVDC (polyvinylidene chloride) high barrier polymers provide exceptional moisture and oxygen resistance. BoPP (biaxially oriented polypropylene) high barrier polymers are lightweight and versatile, used in various applications. Other high barrier polymers may include nylon and polycarbonate, which offer specific benefits for diverse industries.</p></p>
