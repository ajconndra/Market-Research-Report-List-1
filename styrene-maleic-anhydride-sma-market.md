<p><p>The Styrene Maleic Anhydride (SMA) market consists of two primary types: alternate SMA copolymer and random SMA copolymer. Alternate styrene-maleic anhydride copolymers have a regular, alternating arrangement of styrene and maleic anhydride units, resulting in distinct properties like enhanced thermal stability and chemical resistance. In contrast, random SMA copolymers have a more irregular arrangement, leading to different solubility and adhesion characteristics. Both types find applications in coatings, adhesives, and plastics, catering to various industrial needs.</p></p>
