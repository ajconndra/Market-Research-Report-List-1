<p><strong>The Agriculture Pumps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Centrifugal Pump</li><li>Mixed-Flow Pump</li><li>Axial-Flow Pump</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The agriculture pumps market comprises several types tailored for varying irrigation and water management needs. Centrifugal pumps utilize rotational energy to move water, making them efficient for large-scale applications. Mixed-flow pumps combine features of both centrifugal and axial-flow designs, suitable for moderate flow rates. Axial-flow pumps move water along the pump's axis, often used for high-flow, low-head applications. Other market types may include specialized pumps such as submersible or positive displacement pumps, catering to specific agricultural requirements.</p></p>
