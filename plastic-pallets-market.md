HDPE pallets are known for their durability, resistance to chemicals, and ability to withstand extreme temperatures, making them suitable for various industries. PP pallets are lighter, more flexible, and offer excellent impact resistance, ideal for applications requiring frequent handling and transportation. Both types contribute to sustainability by being reusable and recyclable, enhancing logistical efficiency in supply chains across multiple sectors.</p></p>
