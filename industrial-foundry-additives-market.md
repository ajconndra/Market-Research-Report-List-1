<p><p>The Industrial Foundry Additives market comprises three main types: organic additives, inorganic additives, and hybrid additives. Organic additives are derived from carbon-based compounds and are used for enhancing the properties of metal casts, such as improving fluidity and reducing defects. Inorganic additives, sourced from minerals or metals, enhance thermal stability and strength. Hybrid additives combine both organic and inorganic components, optimizing performance by leveraging the benefits of each type, resulting in superior casting qualities and operational efficiency.</p></p>
