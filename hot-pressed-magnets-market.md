<p><strong>The Hot-Pressed Magnets Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Injection Magnets</li><li>Pressed Magnets</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hot-pressed magnets market encompasses two main types: injection magnets and pressed magnets. Injection magnets are produced through a process where magnetic powders are mixed with thermoplastic materials and then injected into molds, resulting in complex shapes and uniform magnetic properties. In contrast, pressed magnets are created by compacting magnetic powders under high pressure and heat, leading to densified materials with excellent magnetic attributes. Both types serve various applications in industries like electronics, automotive, and renewable energy, driving market growth.</p></p>
